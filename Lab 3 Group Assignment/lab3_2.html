Project TrendBetter

TrendBetter is a finance-focused social media platform designed to centralize and organize financial information into a single, scrollable experience. The platform aims to help users stay informed about current financial trends and track market movements. The core problem TrendBetter addresses is the fragmentation of financial content across multiple platforms. News articles, stock prices, social media commentary, and prediction markets are scattered across different sites, making it difficult for users to stay informed efficiently. TrendBetter brings these data sources together into one focused destination.
